How to use this site:

  1. Click on "Edit Inventory" to start adding substances.
  2. In the field labelled start typing the substance name or CAS number.
    You will be given suggestions that match what you've typed.
    (You can use the arrow buttons to navigate the list.)
  3. Add the inventory in tonnes.
  4. Click on "Add to Inventory".
  5. Repeat the previous 3 steps until you've added all the substances in your inventory.
  6. Click on "Done Editing". A new button will appear labelled "Calculate MHI Classification". Click on it.
