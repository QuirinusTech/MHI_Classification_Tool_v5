How it is calculated

The Process:

  • Quantities of all hazardous materials stored or handled at the site are provided by the establishment.
  • Total quantity per hazard class is calculated.
  • The site's MHI classification and Hazard Level is estimated.
  • Report with recommendations is created.

What it can assist in estimating:

  1. Whether the establishment is likely to be classified as a Major Hazard Establishment.
  2. Whether the establishment is likely to be classified as a High Hazard, a Medium Hazard or a Low Hazard establishment.


Note - any classification provided is for information purposes only and should not be interpreted as a formal classification study.
