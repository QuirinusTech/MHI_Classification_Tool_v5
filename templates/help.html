How it works

Quantities of all hazardous materials stored or handled at the site are provided by the establishment. The tool will calculate the total quantity per hazard class and estimate the site's MHI classification and Hazard Level.

The classification tool can assist in estimating:

  1. Whether the establishment is likely to be classified as a Mahor Hazard Establishment.
  2. Whether the establishment is klikely to be classified as a High Hazard, a Medium Hazard or a Low Hazard establishment.
Note - any classification provided is for information purposes only and should not be interpreted as a formal classification study.
